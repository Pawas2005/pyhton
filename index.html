What is an interactive calendar?


An interactive online calendar is a calendar where you can simply jot events into the date they're happening to ensure that you don't miss or forget anything.


Why should you use Interactive Calendar?

Not everyone is as gifted as others when it comes to scheduling and remembering things. We can’t all be perfect. And, even if you are perfect, it can be such a burden
to have to remember everything. An interactive calendar may be the perfect solution for you and your team.
